# Institutional Stress Tests

This document evaluates constitutional resilience
under crisis scenarios.

## Emergency Authority Continuity Assumption

All institutional stress-test scenarios involving emergency defensive authority
must assume continuity of constitutional structure.

Emergency authority does not suspend:

• legislative oversight authority
• judicial review authority
• operation of representative institutions
• transparency obligations except where operational necessity strictly requires

Legislative review and judicial review remain continuously operative during
emergency conditions.

Stress-test modeling must evaluate executive emergency action within the
existing constitutional hierarchy rather than treating emergency authority as
a temporary transfer of sovereign or superior governing power.
